Digital Surgery Solution Features

  • Detect and highlight connective tissue
  • Detect and highlight nerves
  • Detect and highlight pancreas
  • Detect and highlight blood vessels
  • Provide guidance on location of blood vessels early during surgery and by this help to reduce unintentional injury of blood vessels and related complications
  • Detect and highlight ureter
  • Automatic recording of bleeding scenes that meet certain pre-defined conditions for post-surgery
  • Help by raising awareness for a natural tissue plain to follow during dissection which is usually avascular and aneural. This can help to avoid unintentional injury to critical structures.
  • Customization of transparency level of overlays
  • Provide guidance on location of nerves early during surgery and by this help to reduce unintentional injury of nerves and related complications
  • Detect and highlight vas deferens
  • Provide guidance on location of vas deferens early during surgery and by this help to reduce unintentional injury of vas deferens and related complications
  • Customization of persistency of overlays
  • Help to identify the pancreas and to differentiate the pancreas from fatty tissue to help avoid unintentional injuries to the pancreas
  • Provide guidance on location of ureters early during surgery and by this help to reduce unintentional injury of ureters and related complications
  • Automatic detection and recording of clips of bleeding at a specified volume threshold, to review either intra- or post-operatively to identify source and cause of bleeding
  • Transparency level of overlays can be manually adjusted by the user
  • Overlays can be shown at time intervals (e.g., show every 3 seconds)
